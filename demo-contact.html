Contact us for rates

Depending on the work needed, we charge an hourly rate or a daily rate. An estimate will be provided at the beginning of our work, outlining estimated work hours, materials costs, and other expenses. We require 50% deposit before beginning work to allow for purchase of necessary materials, and will bill for the remaining work at the end. Our technicians keep detailed logs of the time they spend on your collection, and will provide a detailed work log at the end of your project. Should we encounter an unexpected expense while working on your project, we will notify you immediately, make recommendations, and come to a solution that will fit your budget.
