About Us

Kristi Moore, CEO

Kristi has 15 years of experience in research, writing and digital file management. She has two years of specialized experience in document and paper conservation, with a bachelor's degree in Historic Preservation and Anthropology from the University of Mary Washington. Her education includes one year overseas study of anthropology and archaeology at the University of Cambridge, UK, internships with the Library of Congress and the Fredericksburg Circuit Courthouse Archives, training in archaeological collection processing with the Veteran's Curation Program, and training from The Mission Continues Fellowship program at the Library of Congress Packard Campus.

Mrs. Moore served in the U.S. Army from 2001-2005, and deployed to Baghdad in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom. She spent the subsequent decade as a DoD analyst focusing on issues in the Middle East and South America. Moore Archives & Preservation LLC is a certified SWaM located in Fredericksburg, VA.
