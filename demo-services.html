Services

Books

At our facility we offer cleaning, page mending, documentation and rehousing. We also work with the finest local bookbinders to provide binding repair and rebinding services.

Documents

We have experience in handling documents up to 330 years old, in varying states of disrepair. Using pH neutral conservation methods, we can reconstruct your document from fragments, stabilizing the structure for further handling needed to digitize and store.

Digitization

Collections are photographed or scanned to provide a digital record of their contents and extent of damage (if applicable). This is vital for inventory and insurance records, and for making your collection available online to a wider audience.

Photographic Media

MAP offers cleaning, rehousing, documentation and digitization of photographic media- including slides and negatives. Images will be optimized for personal or web use, with services such as watermarks, resizing, or touch-up available.

Documentation

Most items in a collection are associated with acquisition paperwork, records of ownership, or a finding aid. If you need any of these, we can provide assistance in creating them. If you have a database for tracking items in your collections, we can perform data entry and other necessary data input to support it.

Archives Program Establishment

Offering a free survey and estimate of your collection, we can help you establish basic policies and structures for effective retrieval and item tracking. We offer full rehousing of you collection and finding aids, to ensure the most logical storage solutions to facilitate the archives' use.
